Returns & Exchanges

Return Policy

At ROVI, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, we offer a hassle-free return and exchange policy.

Return Window

You have 30 days from the date of delivery to return or exchange your items. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in their original condition with all tags attached.

How to Return

  1. Contact our customer service team at support@rovi.com or call +1 (555) 123-4567 to initiate a return.
  2. You will receive a return authorization number and shipping instructions.
  3. Package your item(s) securely in the original packaging if possible.
  4. Ship the item(s) back to us using the provided return label.
  5. Once we receive and inspect your return, we will process your refund within 5-7 business days.

Return Conditions

  • Items must be in original condition with tags attached
  • Items must not be worn, washed, or damaged
  • Original packaging should be included when possible
  • Sale items and clearance items are final sale and cannot be returned
  • Customized or personalized items cannot be returned

Refunds

Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used for the purchase. Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error or a defective item.

Exchanges

We offer exchanges for different sizes or colors, subject to availability. Please contact our customer service team to arrange an exchange.

Contact Us

For any questions about returns or exchanges, please contact us at:

Phone: +1 (555) 123-4567
Hours: Monday - Friday, 9 AM - 6 PM EST
